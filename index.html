Fun Run

Help raise donations to support the Child Crisis Center.

Ezra Lamoreaux, of Boy Scout troop 388, will be holding a family fun run on Saturday, November 14th, 2015 in order to collect baby care items for the Child Crisis Center. Please come and join Ezra for a one mile jog through Sossaman Estates. If running isn't your thing just come to drop off donations and enjoy a pancake breakfast. Any and all donations are greatly appreciated.

14 Nov 2015 - 8AM - Queen Creek
